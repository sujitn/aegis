Why Kids Need Protection with AI

AI chatbots are powerful tools, but they come with real risks for children

Unregulated Screen Time

Kids can chat with AI late at night, bypassing traditional screen time limits or pretending to do homework while engaging in endless conversations.

Unsafe Topics

Children's natural curiosity can lead them to inappropriate content without warning. AI can provide detailed responses on topics they're not ready for.

Jailbreak Tricks

Widely shared prompts teach kids how to trick AI into ignoring its own safety filters, bypassing built-in protections that were meant to keep them safe.

Emotional Blindspots

Even when content isn't explicitly unsafe, emotional distress signals can be missed. AI doesn't alert parents when a child shares concerning feelings.

Everything You Need to Keep Your Family Safe

Comprehensive protection without compromising privacy

Privacy-First Architecture

All classification and filtering happens on your device. No prompts, responses, or usage data ever leaves your computer.

Two Interception Modes

Choose browser extension for simple setup, or MITM proxy for complete coverage of all apps and devices on your network.

Per-Child Profiles

Create individual profiles with different rules for each child. Link profiles to OS user accounts for automatic switching.

Tiered AI Classification

Fast keyword matching catches obvious issues. ML-based analysis provides nuanced understanding of context and intent.

Time-Based Rules

Set daily usage limits and allowed hours. Perfect for managing screen time and ensuring healthy AI usage habits.

Content Category Filtering

Block specific content types: jailbreaks, violence, adult content, self-harm, and more. Customize for each profile.

Emotional Wellbeing Monitoring

Detect concerning patterns like depression, anxiety, or self-harm discussions. Get alerts to stay informed about your child's needs.

Parent Dashboard

Review activity logs, manage profiles, and configure rules from a clean, intuitive interface. Export data anytime.
